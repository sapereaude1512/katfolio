Software solution for
cutting machine

Client: Sormac ; Foodcons

|

Time frame:

2023-2024

Overview

FS-3600 machine allows precise cutting of fruits and vegetables into wedges, slices, or sticks, depending on the desired size. The software, implemented on a Raspberry Pi, facilitates the seamless operation of machine.

My role

I played a key role in designing and developing the client side of the software for this machine. Our software was presented this year on fair in Berlin and fair in Cologne. It gained a great amount of positive feedback. In parallel with this, I developed a UI Kit with scalability in mind, which greatly impacted the speed of product development and shortened the time to market entry.

Reduced 2x the training time for factory workers

Attracted attention at two major fairs in Germany

Sped up 2x total cutting time of products

Implemented feedback to improve 10+ features

Achieved 22% of reduction in development time
